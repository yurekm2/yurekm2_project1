Frequently Asked Questions

Question Answer
1. Why is it called 'The X' anyways? 'The X,' though commonly misconstrued as a reference to the movie "Project X," is actually named that because it comes after the W (from UNLV Football).
2. When should I show up to The X? About an hour and a half after the end of UNLV Football is a good time to arrive. This is roughly 6 hours after a scheduled football start time.
3. Do I have to pay to get in? Partygoers unless otherwise told by the host are expected to pay a $5 cover fee to cover drinks, food, and games. Bringing supplies is also sufficient for a cover.
4. How long does The X usually last? The X will tend to go anywhere from 1:00 to 3:00AM, depending on the start time.
5. What if I can't get home safe? Safe rides are able to be arranged by the host, there is also limited availability for overnight stays.
6. Do I have to be invited to The X? Can I bring friends? The X is invite only. Additional friends are typically allowed but must be known in advance with the host.
7. What happens if UNLV Football loses? The party must go on. That night's party will therefore be known as "The M" (it comes after the L).
8. How many people are at a typical X? Capacity for The X is around 50 people, and is usually met or a bit under.
9. Where is the bathroom? The bathroom is the door directly at the top of the stairs.

DISCLAIMER: THE X DOES NOT CONDONE ANY ILLEGAL OR IRRESPONSIBLE ACTIVITY. PLEASE PARTY RESPONSIBLY. MY UNCLE IS A LAWYER.
